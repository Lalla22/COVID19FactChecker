COVID-19 Fact Checker

Don't be a victim of misinformation.

This is a website where you can upload screenshots of COVID-19 related news, articles, or social media posts to get them fact-checked!

Our goal is to reduce the impact of misinformation and fearmongering on social media.

It's extremely important for people to understand what is happening, and what they can do to protect themselves, and the people around them.


This website was made by Lalla, Carrie and Crystal during the 2020 Technica Hackathon. Please enjoy!
